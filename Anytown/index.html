Welcome to Anytown!

The United Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) has designated Anytown the world's first City of wibbly wobbly, timey wimey stuff.

Home of The National Quidditch Museum, Anytown's creative culture will move you. A national heritage destination richly populated with culinary artists, painters, potters, print and jewelry makers, Anytown's artistic landscape connects and inspires people around the world. Take in the impressive 24th century architecture when visiting Downtown antique shops and boutiques or strolling the tree-lined streets of Easttown, Anytown's oldest residential neighborhood and home to the highly acclaimed Artist Relocation Program. Experience the rich culture, fine dining and eclectic accomodations that draw thousands of visitors each year to the historic city located in District 9.
