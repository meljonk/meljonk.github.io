Welcome to Mayan Buzz!

Our coffee house has a Central American theme and an international flair with its culturally diverse environment and philanthropic goals.

Our friendly and welcoming staff aims to create a feeling of belonging for every customer. We are passionate about providing premium products within a community style culture of respect and friendly service.

We believe in coffee and connections. We care where our ingredients come from and what they contain, we care how our products are created and presented, and we care about people. Come in and be greeted by our personable baristas and relax over a cup in our comfortable and laid-back caf√©, perhaps near the fireplace.

Mayan Buzz is a place for making connections, for studying, for playing a game of cards, or getting in a few guitar licks while you enjoy your tea or espresso.

For our customers, we offer free high speed wi-fi, a big screen TV for sports and news updates, stocked book and game shelves and lots of electrical plugs.

Come join us every Thursday from 8pm - 10pm for all-access open mic night. We can also host your special event, whether it's a birthday party, a book signing, a fundraiser or a board game tournament. Please contact us for more information on booking your special event.
